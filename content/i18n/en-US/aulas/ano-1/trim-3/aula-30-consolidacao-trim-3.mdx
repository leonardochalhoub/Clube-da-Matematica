Workshop with 25 integrating problems combining:

- **Lessons 21-23**: Cartesian plane, distance, lines, parallelism, perpendicularity.
- **Lessons 24-25**: circle, conics (ellipse, parabola, hyperbola).
- **Lessons 26-28**: vectors, dot product, applications in physics.
- **Lesson 29**: 2x2 and 3x3 linear systems.

### Problem style

- **Combined application** (10): geometry + algebra.
- **Modeling** (8): vector physics, telecommunications, trajectories.
- **Challenge** (5): Brazilian Olympiad / ITA / IME level.
- **Proof** (2): consolidate rigorous demonstration.
